Membership routes for 2021

If you are not sure which route you qualify for then email admin@clydewindsurfing.co.uk 

Membership will allow you to participate in SUP or Windsurfing, depending on which course/qualification each member has.

  • If you go on a SUP course, then membership allows you to SUP. 
  • If you go on a Windsurf course, then membership allows you to Windsurf
  • If you go on both courses then, membership allows you to both SUP & Windsurf
  • Club night numbers are limited and are a mix of Kit Hire and Use your own Kit spaces. 
    These nights are Pay per Session

Costs

  • Adult membership is £30
  • Junior membership is £30
  • Family membership is £60

Kit hire on Club Nights

  • SUP £10
  • Windsurf £6

Coached Club Nights

  • SUP £6 with own kit or included in Kit Hire
  • Windsurf £6 with own kit or included with Kit Hire

Social Nights

  • SUP £0            + any kit hire needed
  • Windsurf £0  + any kit hire needed

Club Nights

  • Monday Nights
    • Junior Windsurfing
    • Adult SUP
  • Thursday Nights
    • Junior, Adult (Family) Windsurfing
    • Adult SUP
